About Me

Hello! My name is Joy, and I am an NLP Researcher at Tonative (Nigeria) and currently serve as the Co-Lead for Deep Learning at Data Science Nigeria (OAU). My work focuses on developing language technologies for low-resource African languages, with emphasis on speech recognition, machine translation, and healthcare applications.

I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Linguistics and African Languages from Obafemi Awolowo University. My background in linguistics and computational methods drives my passion for low-resource NLP, tone modeling, and culturally-aware AI.

In 2025, I received a grant to attend the Deep Learning Indaba 2025 in Kigali, Rwanda, where I presented my work ÒWE-YOR and won the Best Poster Award. I was also selected for the AfricaNLP Mentorship Program (ACL).

I am specifically interested in:

  • Preserving African languages through NLP and LLMs
  • Speech recognition and tone modeling
  • Machine translation for healthcare and education
  • Fair and culturally aware AI evaluation
  • Dataset creation for low-resource languages
  • Computational Linguistics
